BIO

Artist Bio

Kam Dridi is a cinematic heavy project built around long-form storytelling, industrial weight, and immersive atmosphere. Echoes Unearthed expands that language into a darker archive: deliberate pacing, excavation imagery, and songs engineered as environments rather than disposable singles.
